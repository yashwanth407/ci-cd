\outl0\strokewidth0 \strokec2 Title: The Last Light
\f1\b0 \
In a village perched on the edge of a vast forest, every evening ended the same way: with the lighting of the Last Lantern. It stood in the center of the town square, ancient and rusted, but it never failed to burn until morning. The villagers believed it held the darkness at bay.\
One evening, just before dusk, a young girl named Lira was chosen to light the lantern for the first time. Her hands trembled as she struck the match. But nothing happened. Again and again, she tried, but the lantern refused to ignite.\
Whispers spread through the crowd. The darkness was coming.\
Then, an old woman stepped forward. She had lived at the forest\'92s edge for as long as anyone could remember. Gently, she knelt beside Lira and whispered, \'93It\'92s not the match. It\'92s the heart.\'94\
Lira closed her eyes and thought of everything she loved\'97her family, the trees, the songs sung during the harvest. She struck the match one final time, and this time, the flame caught.\
The lantern burned brighter than ever before.\
From that night on, the lantern no longer needed matches. It only needed someone brave enough to light it with love.\
